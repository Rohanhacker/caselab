Yes, You Can Make NYCs Best Chocolate Chip Cookie

Posted on 5,28,2015


If there is one chocolate chip cookie everyone should eat while visiting (or, in my case, living in) New York City, it's at Jacques Torres Chocolate. Thin, oversized, chewy, and served warm with dark chocolate oozing from all sides. It's nearly impossible to escape without getting chocolate on your hands and face, but, oh, what a delicious mess. I was so taken by the cookie that I asked Torres for the recipe. Sweet man that he is, he obliged.
